# Product Context: MenaCart

## Overview
MenaCart is a premium multi-vendor clothing e-commerce platform. It connects independent fashion sellers with customers securely and stylishly, solving the operational complexity of order splitting, commission calculation, and returns management.

## Register
register: product

## Users
1. **Customers (Buyers)**: Individuals browsing clothing catalogs, checking out with carts that mix multiple sellers, tracking fulfillment, and requesting returns or exchanges.
2. **Sellers (Vendors)**: Fashion merchants who register, submit KYC documentation, list products, track/fulfill their sub-orders, and manage payouts.
3. **Admins (Platform)**: System administrators managing seller approvals, product verifications, category structure, and payouts.

## Brand Personality & Tone
- **Personality**: Premium, vibrant, stylish, confident, modern.
- **Tone**: Professional, clean, clear, and high-trust. It should feel like a premium utility interface rather than a cheap online catalog.
- **Aesthetic**: Restrained layout with dynamic energy. We use tinted light/dark neutrals with high-contrast typography and a bright, curated OKLCH color palette (e.g., Vibrant Hot Coral) for actions and selections.

## Design Principles
- **Show, don't tell**: Let the products be the hero.
- **Premium feel**: Precise typography (Outfit font), clear visual hierarchy, and breathing room.
- **Clarity**: Make complex multi-vendor logistics feel straightforward and obvious.

## Anti-references
- Bland templates, boring standard SaaS dashboards.
- Cluttered 2000s era e-commerce sites.
- Generic glassmorphism.
- AI-slop layout templates: Avoid generic card repetitions and modals as first thought.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability.
- Clear focus states for keyboard navigation.
- Semantic Colors: Standardized OKLCH colors for hover, focus, active, disabled, success, error, warning, and info.
- Scoped prose line length (65–75ch) for optimal reading.
